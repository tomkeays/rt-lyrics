Acoustic Rarities
Beeswing, 2017

Acoustic Classics II is the eighteenth solo studio album by British singer/songwriter Richard Thompson. It was released by Beeswing Records on 6 October 2017.

1. What If?
2. They Tore The Hippodrome Down
3. Seven Brothers
4. Rainbow Over The Hill
5. Never Again
6. I Must Have A March
7. I’ll Take All My Sorrows To The Sea
8. The Poor Ditching Boy
9. Alexander Graham Bell
10. Sloth
11. Push and Shove
12. End Of The Rainbow
13. Poor Will and the Jolly Hangman
14. She Played Right Into My Hands

All compositions written by Richard Thompson except “Sloth” and “Poor Will and the Jolly Hangman” by Richard Thompson & Dave Swarbrick.
