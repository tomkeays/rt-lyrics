Mr. Riggs Appreciated
Written by Richard Thompson
Appears on Cabaret of Souls 2012

(Keeper)

Sir! Friend! Your hand
I truly truly understand
No remorse seems to linger
Not even in the tippy-tip-tip of your finger
A charming touch of bi-polarity
That goes so well with your morality
Or lack thereof - how utterly divine
I know we're going to get along just fine
